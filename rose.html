Park Chae-young

Roseanne Park (Korean: 박채영; Park Chae-young) was born on 11 February 1997; Born in New Zealand and raised in Australia, better known by the mononym Rosé (Korean: 로제), is a Korean-New Zealand singer and dancer based in South Korea, Rosé signed with South Korean label YG Entertainment following an audition in 2012, training there for four years.
She has an older sister.Rosé initially attended Kew East Primary School graduating in 2009, attending Canterbury Girls' Secondary College in Melbourne for her secondary schooling.She began singing and learned to play guitar and piano as a child and performed in church choir
